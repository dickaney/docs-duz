Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-22  ·  Last updated: 2026-04-22

This Privacy Policy explains how RiskLens ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application and web service. By using RiskLens, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — Email address and password hash when you create an account or sign in.
  • Usage data — App interactions such as screens viewed and features used, collected in aggregate and anonymized form.
  • Device information — Device type, operating system version, and advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID) used for ad delivery on mobile platforms.
  • Log data — IP address, access timestamps, and error logs for service operation and security purposes.

We do not collect full article content, financial data, or any sensitive personal information beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the RiskLens service, including authentication and personalized content access.
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob (mobile app only). The web version does not show AdMob advertisements.
  • To improve service quality through aggregated, anonymized usage analytics.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To respond to your support requests or inquiries.

3. Third-Party Services

RiskLens uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob / Google Play Services — Used to serve banner advertisements on iOS and Android. AdMob may collect your advertising ID and device information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
    Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Supabase — Used for user authentication and database storage. Your email address is stored in Supabase's infrastructure.
    Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
  • OpenAI — Used to generate AI summaries of industry news articles. Only article titles and excerpts (not user data) are sent to OpenAI.
    Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

4. Advertising and Opt-Out

On mobile devices, RiskLens displays banner advertisements powered by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests using your device's advertising identifier.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → turn off "Personalized Ads"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

Opting out will result in non-personalized ads being shown instead. Ad frequency is not affected.

5. Data Retention

Account data (email address) is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us at the address below, after which your account data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Anonymized usage logs may be retained for up to 12 months for service improvement purposes.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), hashed password storage, and Row-Level Security policies on our database. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy
